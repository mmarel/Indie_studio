"Fun, enjoyable, nervous"

Gauntlet takes over everything that made the success of the famous game Bomberman, adding to its sauce unpublished elements. Forget the cosplay bombs and watches, place spells, traps, and skeletons! Alone or several, face your friends or fight artificial intelligences!.

By players, for players

Our team has been able to combine its technical expertise and experience in the field of puzzle and action to create the best for you.

An open source project

Bauntlet is above all an open source project, relying on the actions of its community to develop constantly. New features and content are added regularly. Whether you want to participate in its development or to recover the project to develop it on your side, do what you like!

The Team

The team is composed of 6 students epitech currently in 2nd. During 6 weeks, this team has worked hard to offer you a game experience that we hope will satisfy you! We would also like to thank the epitech pedagogy for their support and advice during the implementation of this project.
